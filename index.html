What is Cashmere?

Cashmere is often called the jewel of natural fibers, and for good reason. It is nature's softest luxury: feather-light, exceptionally warm, and incredibly gentle on the skin. Spun from the fine undercoat of Himalayan goats and handcrafted in Nepal, each piece carries a quiet elegance rooted in tradition, care, and timeless comfort.
